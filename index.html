About Us

Eco-Friendly Car Detailing in Etobicoke – Serving Toronto and GTA At Like New, we specialize in high-quality, eco-friendly car detailing in Etobicoke, proudly serving Toronto and surrounding areas. Using advanced waterless technology, we deliver exceptional results while saving thousands of liters of water. From deep interior cleaning to paint protection, engine bay detailing, and ceramic coating, our expert team ensures your car looks and feels like new. Whether you're in Etobicoke or anywhere in the GTA, trust Like New for premium, sustainable car detailing near you.
